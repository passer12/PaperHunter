üìù Examples

Search for "vulnerability" or "malware" papers in NDSS and USENIX Security (2021‚Äì2024):
paperhunter -con ndss security -year 2021-2024 -kw "vulnerability" "malware"
Search IEEE S&P 2023 papers that must include "attack" and at least one of ["IoT", "firmware"]:
paperhunter -con sp -year 2023 -kw "IoT" "firmware" -kw_all "attack"
Find papers in CCS from 2020 to 2022 related to "side channel":
paperhunter -con ccs -year 2020-2022 -kw "side channel"
Search all supported conferences for "DNS hijacking" in 2024:
paperhunter -con all -year 2024 -kw "DNS hijacking"
